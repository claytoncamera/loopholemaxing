## 5. ACCUMULATION AND DISTRIBUTION PERIODS

### Framework
Bitcoin cycles typically follow the Wyckoff Method's four phases:
1. **Accumulation** — Smart money builds positions at low prices; range-bound, low volume
2. **Markup** — Price begins uptrend; early adopters and trend followers join
3. **Distribution** — Smart money exits into strength at high prices; topping range
4. **Markdown** — Trend reversal; bear market; retail exits

## 7. KEY ON-CHAIN INDICATORS BY CYCLE

### MVRV Z-Score (Market Value to Realized Value)
- **Above 7:** Historical extreme overbought / distribution zone (cycles 1–3 peaks)
- **3–7:** Bull market territory
- **0–3:** Fair value / early bull accumulation
- **Below 0 (negative Z-score):** Deep accumulation zone / historical bottoms
- **Cycle 4 note:** Peak MVRV Z-Score did NOT reach historical overbought levels (stayed below 3.2 band); attributed to ETF institutional dampening

### NUPL (Net Unrealized Profit/Loss) Historical Extremes
- **Euphoria/Greed (>0.75):** Characterized all prior macro tops
- **Capitulation (<0):** Characterized all macro bottoms (BTC trades at aggregate loss)
- **2022 bottom:** NUPL briefly went negative, confirming capitulation

### Puell Multiple
- Measures miner revenue relative to historical average
- **>3.5:** Historical distribution/top signal
- **<0.5:** Historical accumulation/bottom signal
- Miner capitulation (Puell Multiple extreme low) typically coincides with cycle bottoms

### Miner Capitulation Events (Bear Market Signals)
