About the Universal Analyst Engine

UAE is a multi-domain narrative intelligence platform that continuously ingests news and media, analyzes narrative patterns, and scores entities across sports, brand, entertainment, politics, and macro economics. It transforms raw news flow into structured, ranked intelligence.

Capabilities

🌐
Multi-Domain Intelligence
Tracks 92+ entities across 5 domains with continuous monitoring.
📡
25+ Data Sources
ESPN, Billboard, CoinDesk, TechCrunch, Politico, Reuters, and more.
🧠
Narrative Analysis
42,000+ narratives analyzed for sentiment, momentum, and fragility.
📊
Scoring Engine
Weighted scoring with narrative sentiment and momentum analysis.
⏱
Near Real-Time
Data refreshes continuously. Dashboard auto-refreshes every 30s.
🎯
Confidence Scoring
Every score includes confidence based on article volume and diversity.

Methodology

Narrative Sentiment (brand, entertainment, politics, macro): Weighted composite from average sentiment (70%), positive ratio (15%), and negative ratio (15%) of matching articles over 7 days.

Momentum (sports): Measures narrative velocity — buzz and coverage relative to peers.

Confidence derived from article count and source diversity.
